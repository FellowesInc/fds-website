Vertical Market Contract Updates

In case you missed it, these are the updates that were made to Fellowes' contracts since the last newsletter. 


~ Massachusetts Higher Education Consortium (MHEC)~ 

Fellowes has been awarded new MHEC contract #MC13-C07, effective 1/1/2026 – 12/31/2030.  Contract #MC13-C07 replaces contract #MC12-C07, which expired 3/31/2026.  


The State of Connecticut has terminated all furniture and walls contracts and is now teamed up with MHEC. Our new MHEC contract replaces our previous walls-only contract with the State of Connecticut, allowing them to purchase both furniture and walls from Fellowes. 


New ordering documents are available on the Vertical Markets Page of our website. Reach out to the Vertical Markets team for more information.
